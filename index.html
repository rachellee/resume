My Story

I started to teach myself to code when I was eleven years old from a desire to use pretty hovers and sparkles on my Xanga and MySpace pages to make the "coolest layout ever". Fast forward thirteen years, a college degree, and a lot of soul searching, I realized that I had already discovered my true passion thirteen years ago.

I have a deep love of design and a strong desire to make my clients a product that they love and go above-and-beyond to make sure that they are happy. I am service-oriented, detail-oriented, ambitious, and curious.
