My Story

I started to teach myself to code when I was eleven years old from a desire to use pretty hovers and sparkles on my Xanga and MySpace pages to make the "coolest layout ever". Fast forward thirteen years, a college degree, and one deep soul search, I realized that I had already discovered my true passions thirteen years ago.

Since then I have been working as a Freelance Designer, Programmer, and Marketing Consultant as well as an Investment Property Operations Manager.

I have a deep love of design and a strong desire to make my clients a product that they love. I am service-oriented, detail-oriented, ambitious, and curious.

In my free time I like to brew Kombucha, practice calligraphy, take style photographs, garden, and glamp with friends.

Want to know more? I would love to hear from you!
