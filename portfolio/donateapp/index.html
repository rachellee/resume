Challenge

My client wanted a clean, minimal, and informative but succinct launch site for an iPhone app he was building. I worked on this in 2014 before any formal education in UX design strategies and protocol. I built the website myself in HTML5, CSS, and Rails.

Research

I looked at various app sites to determine the information and layout of the site.

Solutions

In the beginning, I was doing a one column design even for the desktop app. However, since the site is so minimal, I decided to go with a 3-column design that would collapse down to vertical display for tablets and smartphones.

Tools Used

The tools I used for this project was Adobe Photoshop, pen & paper, Git, HTML5, CSS3, and Rails.
