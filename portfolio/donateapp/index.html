This is a responsive website I designed and built for a client who wanted a clean, minimal, and informative but succinct launch site for an iPhone app he was building. The tools I used for this project was Adobe Photoshop, pen & paper, Git, HTML5, CSS3, and Rails.

In the beginning, I was doing a vertically stacked design. However, since the site is so minimal, I decided to go with a 3-column design that would collapse down to vertical display for tablets and smartphones.
